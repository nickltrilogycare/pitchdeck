Welcome to the Coordination Partner Programme

You've made a smart decision. You're joining Australia's 2nd largest Support at Home provider — a network of 300+ Coordination Partners managing over $500M in client funding nationwide.

This guide is your orientation. By the end, you'll understand exactly how the partnership works, what Trilogy handles, what you handle, and what your first four weeks look like.

What Is the Partnership?

Trilogy Care partners with purpose-aligned organisations and individuals to deliver Support at Home coordination services to clients across Australia.

Here's the key: Trilogy Care does NOT offer care workers or support services. We handle the administration — funding management, compliance, clinical support, billing, and payments. You do what you do best: coordinate care services for your clients.

In Plain English
  • You find and manage clients
  • You coordinate their care services (personal care, cleaning, nursing, allied health, etc.)
  • Trilogy administers their Support at Home funding
  • Trilogy pays you a coordination fee based on the services you coordinate
  • Everyone wins: clients get more care hours, you earn a sustainable income, Trilogy grows its network

How the Partnership Works

  1. Your Support at Home client contacts you to express interest
  2. You explain they can coordinate their services with Trilogy Care
  3. You complete the referral form
  4. Trilogy works with you and the client through onboarding
  5. Client falls under the Fully Coordinated management tier
  6. Client completes onboarding with Trilogy
  7. Trilogy shares the care plan and budget with you
  8. You schedule and organise services according to the care plan and budget

What Trilogy Handles vs What You Handle

You (Coordinator)

  • Find and recruit clients
  • Build care plans with clients
  • Source workers and service providers
  • Manage rosters and scheduling
  • Coordinate day-to-day care
  • Liaise with families
  • Log incidents and feedback
  • Build your local referral network

Trilogy Care (Provider)

  • Funding administration and claims
  • Compliance and regulatory reporting
  • Worker background checks and onboarding docs
  • Clinical nursing support (phone access)
  • Invoice processing and payments
  • Monthly statements for clients
  • Income Tested Fee collection
  • Care plan reviews and approvals
  • Portal and technology platform

Your Dedicated Partnership Manager

Every Coordination Partner is assigned a dedicated Partnership Manager (PM). Your PM is your main point of contact at Trilogy Care.

What Your PM Does
  • Guides you through onboarding
  • Helps you set up your price book
  • Supports your client acquisition strategy
  • Conducts monthly business reviews
  • Connects you to marketing resources
  • Answers questions and solves problems

Your First 4 Weeks

  • Week 1 Onboarding & Branding Discovery call with your Partnership Manager, sign agreement, submit your branding for your co-branded brochure.
  • Week 2 Learning the System SAH orientation, price book setup, understand the fee structure and coordination fee model.
  • Week 3 Platform Training Portal training, practice care plans, compliance walkthrough, learn the client management tools.
  • Week 4 Go Live Begin warm market outreach, first client introductions, start building your referral pipeline.
