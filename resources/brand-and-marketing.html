Your Brand Identity as a Coordination Partner

As a Trilogy Care Coordination Partner, you're not an employee — you're an independent business with your own brand. The partnership is positioned as:

Your positioning

"[Your Business Name] — Support at Home Coordinator, partnered with Trilogy Care"

This gives you the best of both worlds: your personal brand and local relationships, backed by the credibility of Australia's 2nd largest Support at Home provider.

Approved Messaging

Use these pre-approved messages in your marketing materials, social media, and conversations:

About Trilogy Care

Copy & paste

"Trilogy Care supports older Australians to live well at home for longer, by helping them build the Support at Home plan they want and need."

About your partnership

Copy & paste

"[Your Business Name] partners with Trilogy Care, a registered Support at Home provider, to coordinate and manage your Support at Home. Our role is to support you in managing your home care so you can continue to live confidently at home."

About the value

Copy & paste

"Working with [Your Business Name] and Trilogy Care means you'll spend less on management fees, so more of your budget goes to care and services that help you stay at home longer."

Key selling points to use

  • Affordable care management — less on fees, more on care
  • Transparent pricing — no hidden fees or surprises
  • Flexibility and control — choose your own workers and services
  • Dedicated care partner at Trilogy Care for every client
  • Compliance and reliability — zero sanctions history

Marketing Approval Process

All references to Trilogy Care must be approved before publishing. This includes:

  • Use of Trilogy Care logos
  • References to the Trilogy Care brand
  • References to Trilogy Care services
  • Any marketing materials mentioning the partnership

Process

  1. Send your draft to your Partnership Manager (your first point of contact)
  2. If PM unavailable: email marketing@trilogycare.com.au
  3. Typical turnaround: 2–3 business days
  4. You'll receive either approval or suggested edits
Important

Do not publish any materials referencing Trilogy Care without written approval. This protects both your brand and the partnership.
