The Future Focus x Trilogy Care Partnership Brand

This is an enterprise partnership, not a white-label arrangement. Future Focus retains its own brand identity — trusted by hundreds of NDIS participants across VIC, QLD, and WA — while adding Trilogy Care as a named, credentialled aged care partner behind the service.

The co-brand positioning

"Future Focus — Support Coordination & Aged Care, powered by Trilogy Care"

Your NDIS credibility is your greatest marketing asset. Families who trust Future Focus with complex disability plans will extend that trust to aged care. The positioning makes this natural: same organisation, same values, expanded capability.

Approved Messaging Templates

Use the following across all channels — website, social, collateral, and in conversations. These are pre-approved by both organisations.

Introducing the service

Copy & paste

"Future Focus now coordinates aged care alongside NDIS support. Through our partnership with Trilogy Care — one of Australia's leading Home Care providers — we can help your older family members live well at home for longer. Same dedicated team. Same standard of coordination. Now for the whole family."

For existing NDIS client families

Copy & paste

"You already trust Future Focus to coordinate [client name]'s NDIS supports. We can now help your parents or older relatives access government-funded aged care at home. Future Focus handles the coordination; Trilogy Care manages compliance, billing, and the government portal. You don't have to navigate it alone."

For GP and hospital collateral

Copy & paste

"Future Focus coordinates aged care services for older Australians through a partnership with Trilogy Care. Our coordinators manage the full Support at Home journey — from My Aged Care assessment to ongoing care planning. We have capacity across [location] and welcome referrals from your practice."

Leveraging NDIS Credibility in Aged Care Marketing

NDIS coordination is widely understood to be the most complex case management work in Australia. Use this reputation directly in your aged care marketing — it sets Future Focus apart from traditional aged care providers.

Key differentiator message

"We've navigated 18-month NDIS audits, complex behaviour support plans, and multi-agency funding arrangements. Aged care coordination is something we do well."

This is not arrogance — it is a factual skills statement that GPs, discharge planners, and families will immediately appreciate.

Key selling points to use

  • Proven coordination expertise — 300+ NDIS participants already under Future Focus management
  • Backed by Trilogy Care — 500M+ FUM, zero compliance sanctions, government-registered provider
  • Whole-family support — one organisation for disability and aged care
  • Dedicated team — Future Focus coordinators supported by Trilogy Care admin and technology
  • Local, across VIC, QLD, and WA — nine locations, community-based coordination

GP and Hospital Collateral Strategy

GPs and hospital discharge planners are the highest-value referral sources for aged care. Future Focus coordinators already know how to build these relationships through NDIS work — the approach is the same.

Channel What to Leave Follow-Up Cadence
GP Practice Co-branded brochure (5–10), business card for local coordinator, referral pathway one-pager Quarterly in-person visit to replenish and maintain relationship
Hospital Discharge Unit Co-branded brochure, coordinator business card, "How to refer" card with phone number Monthly visit; build relationship with social worker and discharge planner
Allied Health (OT, Physio) Brochure, business card, note that you also coordinate NDIS supports Quarterly; emphasise cross-referral opportunity (they refer aged care, you refer NDIS)
Retirement Villages Brochures at reception, ask about notice boards or resident newsletters Bi-annual visit; offer to present at a resident information session
