Revenue & Pricing Guide

10 min read

How You Earn

As a Coordination Partner, you earn a coordination fee based on the services you coordinate for your clients. This is a percentage of the service value — typically 10–15%, agreed in your price book with Trilogy.

How it works

  1. Your client has a Support at Home budget (e.g., $29,696/year for Classification 4)
  2. You coordinate services — personal care, cleaning, nursing, allied health, etc.
  3. Trilogy claims the service cost from the government
  4. Trilogy pays you a coordination fee (your agreed %) on the services you coordinated
  5. Payment is automatic via RCTI (Recipient Created Tax Invoice) — monthly to your bank
Key Advantage Over NDIS Hourly Billing

Your revenue scales with your portfolio. More clients = more services coordinated = more fees. No cap, no rate freeze, no billable hours ceiling.

The Comparison
  • NDIS SC at Level 2: $100.14/hr × 25 billable hrs/wk × 48 weeks = ~$120K ceiling
  • Aged care CP with 50 clients: $189K – $284K (50–135% above the NDIS ceiling)
  • And aged care clients require half the coordination time — you manage double the caseload

Client Contributions (Means-Tested Fees)

Some clients pay an Income Tested Fee based on their financial situation, assessed by Services Australia.

  • Full age pensioners: generally little or no ITF
  • Part pensioners: moderate ITF
  • Self-funded retirees: higher ITF (but still capped)
  • Commonwealth Seniors Health Card holders: reduced ITF

You don’t calculate this. Trilogy and Services Australia handle it. The ITF reduces the government subsidy, but the client’s care plan remains the same.

What to Tell Clients About Fees

Script Template

“Trilogy Care charges a care management fee of [X]% of your Support at Home budget. This covers all administration, compliance, clinical support, and your care coordinator. The rest of your budget goes directly to the care services you choose.”

Key messages:

  • Less on fees, more on care
  • Transparent — no hidden charges
  • Monthly statement shows exactly where every dollar goes

How RCTI Works

RCTI = Recipient Created Tax Invoice. Instead of you invoicing Trilogy, Trilogy creates the invoice on your behalf and pays you automatically.

  • Payments: monthly to your nominated bank account
  • Timeline: paid within 5–7 business days of month end
  • Statement: detailed breakdown of services coordinated, clients, and fees
  • GST: handled automatically
No Chasing Invoices

In NDIS, you invoice plan managers and wait. With Trilogy, the payment process is fully automated. You coordinate the care, and the money arrives in your account every month — no invoicing, no chasing, no delays.
