The 3-Step Switching Process

Switching providers is simpler than most people expect. Here are the three steps.

1
Notify Current Provider
  • Client (or you on their behalf) calls their current provider
  • Give notice of intent to transfer — typically 14 days notice required
  • Agree on a termination date
  • Request a final statement of unspent funds
Tip

Be polite and professional — the aged care community is small. A respectful exit leaves the door open for future referral relationships.

2
Contact Trilogy Care
  • Call 1300 459 190 or email info@trilogycare.com.au
  • Provide: client name, DOB, My Aged Care reference number
  • Trilogy handles all transfer paperwork with Services Australia
  • Unspent funds transfer automatically to Trilogy
  • No gap in funding — it’s a seamless transition
3
Welcome to Trilogy Care
  • Client meets their dedicated Care Partner at Trilogy
  • You (the coordinator) work with the client to develop their care plan
  • Services begin under the new arrangement
  • Client can keep their existing care workers in most cases

What to Tell the Client

Here are the key talking points to reassure clients during the switching conversation.

Script / Talking Points

“You have the right to switch providers at any time — it’s your choice.”

“Your funding follows you. You won’t lose anything. Any unspent money transfers to Trilogy Care automatically.”

“We’ll handle all the paperwork with your current provider and Services Australia. You don’t need to do much at all.”

“The whole process usually takes 2–3 weeks. During that time, your existing services can continue as normal.”

“You can keep your current care workers if you’d like — in most cases they can continue under the new arrangement.”

Key Reassurance

The biggest fear clients have is losing their funding or having a gap in care. Address this upfront: funding transfers automatically, and services can continue uninterrupted during the transition period.

Frequently Asked Questions

“Will my client lose any funding?”

No. Unspent funds transfer from the old provider to Trilogy Care. There’s no gap and no loss.

“How long does the switch take?”

Typically 2–3 weeks from the client giving notice to their current provider.

“Does my client need to do anything?”

Very little. They notify their current provider (or you can help). Trilogy handles the Services Australia paperwork. Client signs a new agreement with Trilogy.

“Can they keep their current care workers?”

In most cases, yes. Workers can continue providing services through Trilogy’s platform. Compliance checks may need to be transferred.

“What if the current provider makes it difficult?”

Clients have the legal right to switch providers at any time. If there are any issues, Trilogy’s team can assist with the transition.

“What about clients on legacy Home Care Packages?”

Same process. Legacy HCP clients transitioning to Support at Home go through the same 3-step switching process.
