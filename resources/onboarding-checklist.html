Your Onboarding Journey

This checklist walks you through your first four weeks as a Trilogy Care Coordination Partner, plus the ongoing monthly rhythm that keeps your business growing.

Ongoing
1
Discovery & Setup
Lay the foundations for your partnership
  • Complete discovery call with your Partnership Manager
  • Review and sign the partnership agreement
  • Submit your business details (ABN, business name, contact info)
  • Submit your logo for co-branded brochure
  • Receive Trilogy Care Portal login credentials
  • Read the Getting Started Guide
2
Learning & Pricing
Understand the programme and your revenue model
  • Read Support at Home Explained guide
  • Review and finalise your price book with PM
  • Understand the Fully Coordinated fee structure
  • Read Roles & Responsibilities guide
  • Read Revenue & Pricing Guide
  • Familiarise yourself with the SAH funding classifications
3
Training & Systems
Get hands-on with the platform and your marketing assets
  • Complete Trilogy Care platform onboarding training
  • Practice creating a care plan in the portal
  • Review compliance requirements with PM
  • Receive your co-branded brochure (digital + print-ready)
  • Read Client Acquisition Playbook
  • Read Your Brand in Market guide
4
Go Live
Start building your pipeline and landing your first clients
  • Begin warm market outreach (parents of NDIS clients)
  • Send therapist network email (template in Client Acquisition Playbook)
  • Schedule your first hospital discharge unit visit
  • Drop brochures at 3–5 GP practices
  • Set up your first social media posts (templates in Brand guide)
  • Complete your first client referral (if applicable)
∞
Ongoing Monthly Checklist
The rhythm that sustains and grows your business
  • Monthly business review with Partnership Manager
  • Update pipeline tracker
  • Monthly hospital visit (maintain discharge planner relationships)
  • Review client statements and payments
  • Post 2x LinkedIn + 3x Facebook per week
  • Quarterly GP practice visits
  • Continue community outreach cadence
  • Review revenue vs targets
Pro Tip

Print this checklist and stick it on your wall for the first month. The partners who follow this sequence consistently are the ones who hit 10 clients fastest. Your Partnership Manager will check in on your progress at each step.
