1
Executive Discovery & Capacity Assessment
Align on partnership terms, capacity, and client allocation strategy
  • Executive alignment session — Luke Traini + Future Focus CEO/leadership to confirm partnership intent and terms Luke FF Leadership
  • Capacity assessment — Future Focus provides coordinator headcount per location, caseload capacity, and current NDIS client volume FF Leadership
  • 50 foundation client strategy agreed — which locations, which client profiles, warm vs cold pipeline split Luke FF Leadership
  • Commercial terms confirmed — 10–15% coordination fee model, revenue share, reporting cadence Luke Nick
  • Dedicated team introductions — Jake Kelly-Grubb introduced to Future Focus leadership as day-to-day Partnership Manager Jake
  • Data room access — Future Focus reviews Trilogy Care compliance documentation, FUM overview, and provider credentials Jake
2
Partnership Agreement & Client Allocation
Sign documents, finalise legal structure, map 50 foundation clients
  • Partnership agreement executed — legal sign-off both parties Luke FF Leadership
  • 50 foundation clients identified — Future Focus provides list of warm aged care prospects (parents of NDIS clients, known community members) FF Leadership Coordinators
  • Client allocation per location — distribute 50 clients across Future Focus locations proportionate to coordinator capacity Jake FF Leadership
  • My Aged Care referral pathway confirmed — Trilogy linked as registered provider for all Future Focus referrals Jake
  • Co-branding assets briefed — Nick provides brand assets and approves Future Focus + Trilogy Care co-brand usage guidelines Nick
  • Portal credentials prepared — Trilogy portal logins staged for Future Focus coordinator team Jake
3
Team Training & Platform Setup
Coordinators trained, systems live, marketing assets distributed
  • Coordinator induction session — Jake delivers aged care overview to all Future Focus coordinators: Support at Home programme, funding model, care planning Jake Coordinators
  • Trilogy Care platform training — coordinators complete hands-on walkthrough of referral portal, care plan creation, client management Jake Coordinators
  • Sales conversation training — coordinators practise NDIS-to-aged-care language translation using the Aged Care Conversations guide Jake Coordinators
  • Co-branded brochures delivered — per-location print and digital brochures (Future Focus x Trilogy Care) ready for distribution Nick Jake
  • Social media toolkit distributed — social tile concepts, approved messaging, and per-location handles confirmed Nick FF Leadership
  • GP and hospital collateral printed — brochures and referral cards ready for week 4 clinic drops at each location Coordinators
  • Compliance briefing completed — coordinators understand their role boundaries and Trilogy's responsibilities under the Aged Care Quality Standards Jake
4
50 Clients Transfer & Go Live
Foundation clients onboarded, pipeline open, partnership active
  • Foundation client outreach begins — coordinators contact the 50 identified warm prospects using the Aged Care Conversations scripts Coordinators
  • First referrals submitted to Trilogy — any clients who agree to proceed referred via portal or Jake within 24 hours Coordinators Jake
  • Trilogy contacts referred clients — within 2 business days of each referral; SLA monitored by Jake Jake
  • GP and hospital drop visits completed — coordinators visit 3–5 GP practices and local hospital discharge units per location Coordinators
  • First social posts published — each Future Focus location publishes co-branded aged care announcement across LinkedIn and Facebook Coordinators FF Leadership
  • Go-live dashboard review — Luke, Nick, and Jake review pipeline status, referrals received, and 50-client progress Luke Nick Jake
  • First client active — partnership is live Coordinators Jake
∞
Ongoing Partnership Rhythm
Monthly and quarterly cadence to sustain and grow the partnership
  • Monthly partnership review — Jake + Future Focus leadership: pipeline, active clients, revenue, blockers Jake FF Leadership
  • Quarterly executive review — Luke + Nick + Future Focus CEO: strategic health of partnership, growth targets, expansion to new locations Luke Nick
  • Coordinator pipeline reporting — monthly update on referrals, warm prospects, and capacity Coordinators
  • Monthly hospital visits — maintain discharge planner relationships at each location Coordinators
  • Quarterly GP practice visits — replenish brochures and nurture referral relationships Coordinators
  • Client statements and revenue reviewed — Trilogy sends monthly statements; Jake reviews with FF leadership Jake
  • Expansion planning — when 50 client target hit, review capacity for next tranche Luke FF Leadership
The 50-client commitment

Trilogy Care's 50 foundation clients are an investment in the partnership — Trilogy absorbs the cost of onboarding, compliance, and platform setup. The expectation is that Future Focus coordinators actively work toward filling this pipeline in the first 90 days. Jake is your point of contact for everything.
